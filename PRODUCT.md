# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Software engineers, developers, and hackathon builders looking for actionable career opportunities (GitHub issues, Arbeitnow jobs, Devpost hackathons). Their context is high-intent execution: they need to bridge skill gaps, track deadlines, and execute complex, multi-step career projects without getting overwhelmed by static checklists. What they need is a dynamic agent that remembers their progress and adapts their roadmap in real-time.

## Product Purpose
Waypoint AI is an adaptive career agent and roadmap generator built for the Cognee Hackathon 2026. It ingests live opportunities across multiple platforms and constructs customized, step-by-step execution plans. Its primary purpose is to demonstrate deep AI intelligence by leveraging Cognee's knowledge graphs and memory lifecycle (`remember`, `recall`, `improve`, `memify`, `forget`) to continuously learn from user feedback (step rejections, completions) and reorder roadmaps on the fly. Success looks like an unmistakable, intelligent demo where AI adaptation is visible, immediate, and reliable.

## Brand Personality
Intelligent, Adaptive, Decisive.
Voice and tone balance expert technical precision with dynamic responsiveness. It feels like an advanced neural operating system—raw, capable, and deeply informed by graph memory.

## Anti-references
- **Generic AI Slop**: No random purple-cyan gradients, floating glassmorphism, or pointless scroll-fade-rise animations on every element.
- **Fluffy Marketing Sites**: Avoid oversized decorative hero banners without substance or generic two-column feature checklists that don't demonstrate real functionality.
- **Timid, Flat Layouts**: Avoid low-contrast hierarchies where critical AI adaptation events look like ordinary list re-sorts.
- **Shadow Overload**: Zero drop-shadows or elevation layers; depth must come strictly from hairline borders and color contrast against a flat void canvas.

## Design Principles
1. **Highlight Memory Depth**: Make Cognee's graph intelligence and real-time adaptation the undeniable hero of the experience. When an AI adaptation event occurs (like `cognee.improve()`), celebrate it visually with unmistakable signals (e.g., Memify Violet pulse, `adapted` badges).
2. **Color as the Organizing System**: Use color decisively to communicate semantic meaning at a glance before reading a word. Each opportunity type (Issue Green, Job Blue, Hackathon Orange) and step status (Reject Red, Memify Violet) owns its specific visual register.
3. **Flat-Void Restraint**: Maintain an uncompromising dark canvas (`#0e100f` Void Black) with zero elevation or drop-shadows. Structure, rhythm, and separation are created entirely through 1px hairline borders (`Olive Stone`, `Cream Glow`) and typographic contrast.
4. **Storytelling Meets Execution**: Balance compelling brand storytelling on landing surfaces with high-density, frictionless workflow execution in the interactive roadmap app.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG AA Compliance**: Guarantee strict contrast ratios across all text and interactive badges against the Void Black and warm off-white canvases.
- **Motion Restraint**: Full support for `prefers-reduced-motion` across HTML5 canvas grid distortions, GSAP branch narratives, and UI pulse animations, instantly falling back to clean static states.
- **Clear State Differentiation**: Rely on dual-signal feedback (color + mono typographic tags/icons) rather than color alone (e.g., checkbox X in Reject Red, strikethrough-free completion treatments).
